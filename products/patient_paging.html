With Patient Paging from Clockwise.MD, providers can guide patients through every step of their visit, helping to streamline patient flow. Customizable SMS text messages provide instant updates on wait and location status, reducing patient anxiety and improving overall satisfaction levels.

See Patient Paging in Action

Maximize efficiency and minimize stress with simple patient paging.

Large or complex medical facilities can often overwhelm an already stressed patient, leading to confusion and anxiety over the status of their appointment and the appropriate site for care.

Clockwise.MD's Patient Paging system helps patients navigate complex health systems with ease - without the need for bulky and expensive pagers. By sending templated messages to patients to let them know where to be and when, providers are able to maximize operational efficiency, save time, and reduce patient anxiety. These customizable SMS text messages instantly notify patients of the status of their wait, helping to improve patient satisfaction by thoroughly communicating appointment status and location details at the individual patient level.
