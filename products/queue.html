Introducing a "fast pass" for healthcare.

It's no secret that long wait times at medical facilities can severely impact the patient experience - and not in a good way. Unfortunately, those waits can feel even longer when elements like uncertainty or unfairness come into play, causing patients to consider alternatives outside of your practice.

Clockwise.MD's virtual queuing solution empowers urgent cares, emergency rooms, primary and specialty care providers, laboratory clinics, and more to put the patient back in control. By giving patients the ability to self-schedule appointments online and wait from the comfort of their home or a nearby coffee shop, you're creating a patient-centric workflow that has been proven to boost patient satisfaction and increase retention rates.
