Meet the Clockwise.MD Team

Clockwise.MD was founded in 2012 when a group of seasoned healthcare information technology entrepreneurs began applying customer-centric tools used successfully in other industries to the healthcare industry. The goal was to arm providers with the tools they needed to improve the overall patient experience. Today, Clockwise.MD's online scheduling and virtual queuing functionalities are used by more than 1,300 urgent care clinics, emergency departments, primary and specialty care clinics, radiology labs, and more to help boost patient satisfaction, manage online reputations, and improve patient retention.
